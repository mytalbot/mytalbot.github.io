Reports & Power Analyses

Since 2016 I’m offering professional statistical reports (German: Gutachten) for sample size calculations & Power Analyses. The following reports are a selection of the major projects for which I did the power analysis. Please note: in Germany, every experiment using laboratory animals requires a proper power analysis. Most of the following titles are, therefore, in German.
